Our Tea

 Darjeeling's climate is cool here, which is why the location of Darjeeling is characterized
by fog and the tea bushes grow at an angle of 45 degrees. All this contributes to the unique
flavor of Darjeeling tea.
 Darjeeling tea belongs to the highest category of
noble varieties. It is worth saying that Darjeeling is
a city in India, located in the Himalayas.
 It is in honor of the city that the tea was named.
The tea plantations here cover an area of 17500
hectares.

Our Coffee

 Our Coffee Coffee is one of Tanzania's main export products, playing a huge role in the
country's economy.
Coffee production in Tanzania reaches 30-40 thousand tons per year, 70% of the
total volume is accounted for by arabica and 30% by robusta.
 In a cup: Sweet balanced coffee with fruity notes,
with aromas of nuts, chocolate, apple and
sandalwood, in the taste notes of watermelon and
hazelnuts, a long aftertaste.
 Good in espresso, including automatic coffee
machines, a geyser coffee maker and a turkey.
